Emotuo

Emotuo food

Emotuo is a local delicacy in Ghana which is prepared by cooking rice and shaping it into ball like form.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Meat or fish
  • Tomato
  • Pepper
  • Groundnut paste
  • Ginger and garlic
  • Onion
Steps
  1. Put the rice on fire
  2. Stir it for the water in it to dry and for it to become a bit sticky
  3. Pick it from the cooking pot with a small bowl and put it in a coma
  4. Blend the tomato, pepper, ginger and garlic together
  5. Mix the groundnut paste with water and put it on fire
  6. Put the meat and fish on fire
  7. Cut the onion and put them on the meat
  8. Pour the blended mixture into the meat and allow it to steam
  9. Wait for sometime to put the mixed groundnut paste in it
  10. Stir it for sometime and wait for about 15 mins and it will be ready to serve
